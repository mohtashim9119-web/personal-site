// 01 — My Journey

Battle-Tested,
Not Textbook-Trained.

I didn't learn entrepreneurship in a classroom. I learned it in the trenches — with real money on the line, real risk to absorb, and real consequences when things went sideways. Here's the unfiltered version.

The Wins

What worked, and what I built from it

  • ▸ Built and scaled multiple profitable online businesses from scratch — generating consistent, real revenue across different niches and models.
  • ▸ Established a solid freelance IT career serving international clients with infrastructure, networking, and security solutions that actually hold up.
  • ▸ Survived and adapted through every major market shift since 2018 — while competitors folded, I pivoted and kept growing.
  • ▸ Developed a sharp ability to spot digital opportunities early and move decisively — before the market prices them in.

The Losses

The crashes that made me sharper

  • ▸ Launched ventures that burned capital with nothing to show — and absorbed more real education from each failure than any success ever taught me.
  • ▸ Chased trends that evaporated overnight. Paid the price in time and money. Never made the same category of mistake twice.
  • ▸ Trusted the wrong partners. Absorbed hard lessons about vetting people, contracts, IP protection, and when to walk away.
  • ▸ Failed fast, cut losses faster, pivoted without ego. That mindset is the only reason I'm still in the game — and still building.
